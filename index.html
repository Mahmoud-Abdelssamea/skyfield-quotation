Monthly Timesheets & Payroll — كشف الحضور والرواتب

Payroll totals can be posted to the selected project's actual cost.
Net Total is calculated as Attendance Days ÷ 30 × (Basic Salary + Housing & Transport + Site / Car Allowance) + Other Allowance + Overtime Amount + Other Earnings − Deductions. This means the housing and car allowance are prorated with attendance instead of being added in full. Saving the sheet posts one consolidated payroll expense to the selected project's Actual Expenses; saving again updates that same expense instead of duplicating it.

📁 Project Records

🧾 Saved Quotations & Legacy Invoice Quotations

Use this area when one project has more than one station or section. Save each station as a separate quotation, then click Add as Project to convert and link it to a tracked Project. Only after that step will the Create Proforma option appear. The project keeps the quotation, Proforma Invoices, payments, commission, attachments, and documents linked in one place.

📄 Proforma Invoices / الفواتير المبدئية

A Proforma Invoice is a payment-request document and is not a Tax Invoice. It can only be created after the quotation has been converted and linked to a Project. Choose Full, Percentage, or Fixed Amount, then preview and print it as an A4 PDF. Every saved Proforma Invoice is linked to its Project and automatically creates or updates a pending payment entry. Mark that entry as paid inside the Project when payment is received.
